About BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, who co-write or co-produce much of their material.

BTS stands for the Korean phrase Bangtan Sonyeondan (Korean: 방탄소년단; Hanja: 防彈少年團), which translates literally to 'Bulletproof Boy Scouts'. According to member J-Hope, the name signifies the group's desire "to block out stereotypes, criticisms, and expectations that aim on adolescents like bullets".

Members

Kim Seokjin (Joined in 2013) - The oldest member, known for his visual appeal and deep vocals. Jin is often called “Worldwide Handsome” for his charming looks and has a gentle personality. His vocals stand out in songs like "Awake" and "Epiphany."

Back Story: Jin's life was on a different track before BTS. He was a film studies major at Konkuk University, with a passion for photography and acting. Working part-time jobs to make ends meet, Jin never imagined he'd become a K-pop idol. But fate had other plans, and he was scouted by Big Hit Entertainment while on his way to class.

Min Yoongi (Joined in 2013) - Suga, A rapper and producer, known for his introspective lyrics and laid-back demeanor. Suga is also known for his alter-ego, Agust D, through which he releases solo music that speaks deeply to his personal struggles and ambitions.

Back Story: Suga's early days were marked by a fierce dedication to hip-hop. As an underground rapper and producer, he spent years perfecting his craft, often sacrificing sleep and comfort for the sake of his music. His raw talent and unapologetic lyrics eventually earned him a spot in BTS.

Jung Hoseok (Joined in 2013) - J-Hope, A dancer and rapper, known for his vibrant personality and energy. As a skilled dancer, J-Hope plays a vital role in BTS’s choreography and is the group’s main dancer, often credited with adding a unique style to their performances.

Back Story: J-Hope's journey began in the world of dance. A talented dancer from a young age, he trained tirelessly at Gwangnam High School and later joined the dance crew Neuron. His boundless energy and infectious smile caught the attention of Big Hit Entertainment, and he became one of the first members to join BTS.

Kim Namjoon (Joined in 2013) - The leader, a rapper, and the driving force behind BTS’s success. RM (Kim Nam-Joon) is fluent in English and serves as the group's spokesperson for interviews and speeches. His leadership has been integral in shaping the group's global presence.

Back Strory: Before becoming the leader of BTS, RM was an underground rapper known for his sharp lyrics and smooth flow. He spent countless nights honing his craft, performing at local hip-hop clubs and releasing music on SoundCloud. His passion for rap and his natural charisma eventually caught the attention of Big Hit Entertainment.

Park Jimin (Joined in 2013) - A vocalist and dancer, known for his ethereal charm and graceful movements. Jimin's stage presence captivates audiences, and his emotional voice is featured in songs like "Serendipity" and "Lie."

Back Story: Jimin's story is one of perseverance and passion. A top student in modern dance at Busan High School of Arts, he pushed himself to the limit, often practicing for hours on end. His hard work paid off when he was accepted into the prestigious Korea Arts High School, and later, when he joined BTS.

Kim Taehyung (Joined in 2013) - A vocalist with a unique deep voice and a mysterious aura. V (Kim Taehyung) is known for his charismatic personality, experimental music style, and his acting endeavors. His songs like "Stigma" and "Singularity" highlight his vocal range and versatility.

Back STory:V's early life was marked by a love for music and the arts. A talented saxophonist, he spent years honing his skills, eventually becoming a trainee at Big Hit Entertainment. With his soulful voice and captivating stage presence, V was destined to become a star.

Jeon Jungkook (Joined in 2013) - The youngest member and a versatile performer, known for his exceptional singing, dancing, and athletic abilities. Jungkook has earned the title of "Golden Maknae" for his many talents and contributions to BTS’s music and performances.

Back Story: Jungkook's journey began at a young age, when he trained at Busan High School of Arts. With his incredible dance skills and boundless energy, he quickly caught the attention of Big Hit Entertainment. As the youngest member of BTS, Jungkook brought a fresh perspective and infectious enthusiasm to the group.
